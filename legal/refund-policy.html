Refund Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Thank you for purchasing the Wellenzo ADHD Focus Bundle. We are dedicated to providing products that support neuro-affirming productivity and mindfulness. Since our items are digital and delivered immediately upon purchase, we operate under the following refund policy guidelines.

We offer a 30-Day Shame-Free Guarantee on the Wellenzo ADHD Focus Bundle. If you try the templates and planners and find they do not work for your brain, we will issue a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

1. Conditions for a Refund

To request a refund under our Shame-Free Guarantee, please email us at info@wellenzo.com with:

  • Your email address used at purchase
  • Your order number (from your Stripe receipt)
  • A brief note explaining why the system didn't work for you (this helps us improve our templates, but is not used to block your refund).

2. Digital Product Abuse Policy

We reserve the right to deny refund requests if we detect clear signs of product exploitation or automated serial purchasing and refunding. Planners and ebook files downloaded are for your personal use only. Sharing, duplicating, or reselling these files is strictly prohibited under our terms of service.

3. Processing of Refunds

Once your refund request is received and verified, we will process your refund immediately. The refund will be credited back to your original payment card or account via Stripe. Please note that card processors usually take 5–10 business days to post the credit to your account.

4. Chargebacks

If you experience any issues accessing your downloads, please reach out to us directly at info@wellenzo.com first. Opening a chargeback dispute with your bank will delay the resolution process, as banks can take up to 90 days to settle dispute investigations.
