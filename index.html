Why Traditional Systems Fail Your ADHD Brain

Most productivity systems are designed for neurotypical brains. They assume you can force focus with rigid hours, strict calendar dates, and constant willpower.

For a brain with lower dopamine baseline, starting a routine task feels like a stress response. Your prefrontal cortex fails to register the future reward, your amygdala triggers a survival freeze, and you lock up.
