Before you add another tactic, know what can waste your time.

Traditional calendars and rigid micro-scheduling assume consistent daily energy. Trying to force an ADHD brain to follow them often leads directly to task freeze.

1. Strict hour-by-hour calendars

ADHD brains experience fluctuating daily dopamine levels. Attempting to schedule every 30-minute block causes immediate friction and starting anxiety when a single task runs over.

2. Empty page planning guilt

Dated calendars record your inconsistencies. When you take a three-day break, returning to blank dated pages triggers a shame spiral, leading you to abandon the planner.

3. Monolithic to-do lists

Listing tasks linearly without grouping them by cognitive weight overwhelms the prefrontal cortex, triggering amygdala flight response and executive freeze.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this planner different from the 10 others I abandoned?

Traditional planners are dated, meaning if you miss a day, a week, or a month, you are met with empty white space that triggers guilt. The Wellenzo planner is completely undated. If you skip a week, you just turn the page and continue whenever your energy allows—no empty spaces, no shame. Additionally, it structures your day around energy blocks and dopamine appetizers rather than rigid hours.

Do the supplement stacks and concentration guidelines actually work?

We do not promote magic solutions or placebo stacks. The supplement stacking section inside the Playbook focuses exclusively on ingredients and synergies that have been verified in peer-reviewed clinical studies. We analyze stacks like the L-Theanine + Caffeine synergy (for cognitive task speed) and Magnesium L-Threonate (which bypasses the blood-brain barrier for cell energy), detailing accurate dosages, potential side-effects, and clinical limits.

Can I access the bundle on my mobile phone?

Yes. While the PDF planner is hyper-linked and optimized for large screens like iPads and tablets, the Notion Dashboard is built with mobile-responsive templates that fit perfectly on mobile screens. You can add the Notion page to your phone's home screen as a widget, letting you capture thoughts and access your digital Dopamine Menu in less than 3 seconds.
