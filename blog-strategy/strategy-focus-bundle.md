## 4. Internal Linking Map (Maillage Interne)

To pass authority cleanly through the domain, we enforce a strict linking loop:
- **Satellite to Pillar (Upward)**: Every satellite article *must* link back to its respective pillar article using exact or partial anchor text in the first 200 words. (e.g., *"If you are looking for a complete overview, read our [ultimate guide to overcoming ADHD task paralysis](...)"*).
- **Pillar to Satellite (Downward)**: The Pillar article *must* contain a table of contents or dedicated sections linking to each of the 5 satellites.
- **Cross-Silo Linking (Horizontal)**: Limit linking between satellites of different clusters unless logically related (e.g., Sat 2.3 *Workspace Declutter* linking to Sat 1.2 *Dopamine Appetizers* is allowed as environment setup leads to initiation).
