# Creative Brief — Quiet Focus Ebook Cover

- **Brand**: Wellenzo
- **Product**: Quiet Focus (Overcome Task Paralysis & Brain Fog)
- **Author**: Alex, Mental Health & Neurodiversity Creator
- **Visual Palette**: Focus Botanical (Primary `#1A2F25`, Accent `#10B981`, Background `#FAFBF9`, Font **Outfit**)

---

## 🎨 Art Direction: Zen Minimalist Botanical

The visual identity must feel calm, professional, and science-backed. It should completely avoid chaotic layouts or high-stress neon productivity designs. Instead, it leverages a soft, organic aesthetic to signal safety and cognitive relief.

### Specifications
- **Format**: 1600 × 2560 px (Standard Ebook Aspect Ratio 1:1.6)
- **Typography**: **Outfit** (Sans-serif with geometric roundness, matching the premium brand feel).
- **Background**: Soft cream `#FAFBF9` paired with subtle botanical illustrations or clean geometric lines.
